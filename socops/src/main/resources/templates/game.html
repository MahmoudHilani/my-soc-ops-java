Soc Ops

Social Bingo

Break the ice fast, meet new people, and race to five in a row.

How To Play

  • Find people who match the prompts.
  • Click a square when you find a match.
  • Score five in a row to win.

Quick Tips

  • Start with easy squares to build momentum.
  • Move around the room between each match.
  • Watch rows and diagonals as you play.

No setup required. Tap start and play.
